Welcome to GDGUCC

This is an open and non-profit developers group that aims to learn, share and know more about Google technologies.Technologies to be explored include: Android OS, Google Docs, Google Analytics,Google Data APIs and more...

Where we are

UCC GTUG can be located at the University of Cape Coast, Cape Coast, Ghana along the Gulf of Guinea. One of the few universities situated on a coast. A serene atmosphere for studies and learning and also congenial for the exploration and development of talents
